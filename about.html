microSponse is a web app made to inform, help, and understand microaggressions. The complexities of micro behaviors are based on ethnicity, gender, sexual orienation, and more.
Micro events can be triggered by any perception of difference which can include body type, socio-economic class, age)

Still not caught up with with microaggressions? This Atlantic article from 2015 talks about the impact of a microaggression on the writer and how it deeply offended him unintentionally.

microSponse purpose is to educate, aid, and support those who have encountered a microaggression or those who don't even know what a microaggression is and how they can help.



About the Creators


Clara Cabrera, Lily Ng, and Annie Wu are the creators and developers of microSponse. All three of them are 2018 Kode with Klossy scholars and made this web app within two days.

microSponse came about when the group was brainstorming ideas for their final project and Annie brought up the topic of microaggresions and there the designing, code, and development started up.
